About Idaho Bill Briefer

What is this?

Idaho Bill Briefer turns bill text into a structured briefing you can review in minutes. Enter a bill number, and within 3–5 minutes you'll receive a PDF briefing by email.

What's in a briefing?

Each briefing has 7 sections:

  1. Bill Summary — what the bill does and why it's moving
  2. Sponsor Profile — who is driving this bill, their record, and IACI scores
  3. Unintended Consequences — what could go wrong that the bill text doesn't address
  4. Power Flag — does this bill shift authority away from the legislature?
  5. Momentum — is the bill moving or stalled? Trajectory and hearing status
  6. Advocacy Positions — which organizations support or oppose, with coalition alerts
  7. Legislative Activity — sponsors, committee path, and vote record

How it works

  • AI analyzes the full bill text from official legislative sources
  • Demographics come from the US Census Bureau and Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • Legislative data comes from the Idaho Legislature via LegiScan
  • AI is used to analyze and summarize — not to generate facts, statistics, or quotes
