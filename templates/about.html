About Dispatch

What is this?

Dispatch turns bill text into a structured leadership briefing you can review in minutes. Enter a bill number, and within 3–5 minutes you'll receive a PDF briefing by email.

What's in a briefing?

Each briefing has 7 sections:

  1. Bill Summary — what the bill does, the mechanism, and who it affects
  2. Sponsor Profile — who is driving this bill, with legislator scores from IACI, IFF, IFBF, CPAC, and ACLU Idaho
  3. Unintended Consequences — implementation risks and gaps the bill text doesn't address
  4. Governance Impact — does this bill shift authority between branches, agencies, or levels of government?
  5. Momentum — trajectory, pace, hearing status, and next procedural step
  6. Advocacy Positions — organizational support/oppose stances and coalition dynamics
  7. Legislative Activity — sponsors, co-sponsors, committee path, and vote record

How it works

  • AI analyzes full bill text and fiscal notes from official Idaho legislative sources
  • Legislator scores are sourced from IACI, IFF, IFBF, CPAC, and ACLU Idaho scorecards
  • Advocacy positions are pulled from public organizational trackers
  • Legislative history, committee paths, and vote records come from legislature.idaho.gov
  • AI is used to summarize, contextualize, and assess momentum — not to generate facts, statistics, or quotes
