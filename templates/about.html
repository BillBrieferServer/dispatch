About Idaho Bill Briefer

What is this?

Idaho Bill Briefer turns bill text into a structured briefing you can review in minutes. Enter a bill number, and within 3–5 minutes you'll receive a PDF briefing by email.

What's in a briefing?

Each briefing has up to 10 sections:

  1. Bill Snapshot — official bill data (sponsors, status, committee)
  2. Plain-Language Summary — what the bill does in plain English
  3. What the Bill Does — key changes in law or policy
  4. Who and What Is Affected — stakeholders and impacts
  5. Policy Considerations — benefits, concerns, and unknowns
  6. Debate Prep — sample pro and con arguments
  7. Key Questions — questions for committee with possible answers
  8. Uncertainties to Watch — issues that may develop over time
  9. District Profile — how the bill connects to your district's demographics
  10. Legislative Activity — sponsors, committee path, and vote record

How it works

  • AI analyzes the full bill text from official legislative sources
  • Demographics come from the US Census Bureau and Bureau of Labor Statistics
  • Legislative data comes from the Idaho Legislature via LegiScan
  • AI is used to analyze and summarize — not to generate facts, statistics, or quotes
