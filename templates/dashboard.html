How We Identify Sponsors

We cross-reference multiple official sources to identify the most likely sponsor: the Statement of Purpose (SOP) contact, committee minutes (who presented the bill), and floor sponsor designations.

When multiple sources agree, confidence is highest. When only one source is available, we use it but with lower confidence. In the small number of cases where no legislative contact can be identified, the sponsor is shown as “Unidentified.”

This attribution is automated and updated daily. It reflects the most likely sponsor based on official legislative records.
