Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

What Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and name (provided during registration)
  • Authentication data: Password hash (bcrypt), session tokens, trusted device fingerprints
  • Security logs: IP addresses, login attempts, and security events (for fraud prevention)
  • Request history: Bill numbers you request briefers for, timestamps, and job status
  • Cookies: bb_session (authentication) and csrf_session (security token)

Why We Collect It

  • Account management: To authenticate you and deliver briefers to your email
  • Security: To detect unauthorized access, prevent brute-force attacks, and protect your account
  • Service delivery: To generate and deliver bill briefings you request
  • Service improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve the product

Third-Party Services

When you request a briefer, the following data is shared with third-party services:

  • Anthropic (AI analysis): Bill text, fiscal notes, and district demographic data are sent for AI analysis. Your personal information (name, email) is not sent to Anthropic.
  • LegiScan: Bill numbers and session IDs are sent to retrieve official legislative data.
  • IONOS (email delivery): Your email address and the briefer PDF are transmitted for email delivery.
  • US Census Bureau / Bureau of Labor Statistics: Public demographic and economic data is retrieved (no personal data is sent).

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Security logs (login attempts, security events) are retained for 90 days
  • Request history and job files are retained for 90 days after completion
  • Briefer content cache is retained indefinitely (to avoid regenerating previously requested briefers)

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not share your data for advertising purposes
  • We do not track you across other websites
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking scripts

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting info@billbriefer.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact info@billbriefer.com.
